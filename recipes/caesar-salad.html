Ingredientses

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/3 cup Caesar dressing
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets, minced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking stepses

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, grated Parmesan cheese, and croutons.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Caesar dressing, minced garlic, minced anchovy fillets (if using), Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while continuously whisking to emulsify the dressing.
  4. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine, ensuring all the lettuce is evenly coated.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
